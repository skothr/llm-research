\title{
  Training is a multi-stage pipeline, not a single objective: \\
  pre-training to alignment in frontier LLMs, 2017--2026
}
\author{Theory KB --- Paper 2 of 5}
\date{2026-05-04 (draft)}

\begin{document}

\begin{abstract}
Modern frontier-LLM training is a six-stage pipeline: pre-training data
$\to$ mixed-precision and distributed scaffolding $\to$ supervised
fine-tuning $\to$ preference-RL (RLHF/DPO/CAI) $\to$ RL on verifiable
rewards (RLVR/GRPO) $\to$ adaptation and merging. Each stage's
load-bearing variables --- token count, mixture, precision, batch size,
KL constraint, verifier signal, merge geometry --- are now well-
characterized. We argue the pipeline composes; pretending it is one
objective produces persistently confused empirics.
\end{abstract}
