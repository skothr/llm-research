\begin{abstract}
Reasoning gains 2022--2026 are a triangle of (a) inference-time compute
scaling (chain-of-thought, self-consistency, best-of-$N$), (b) verifier
signals at inference (process reward models, MCTS, tree search), and
(c) RL on verifiable rewards using those signals (RLVR, GRPO, DAPO).
Strong reasoning models co-design all three. Isolating any one
understates the gains; pretending reasoning is one mechanism produces
the wrong predictions about scaling.
\end{abstract}
